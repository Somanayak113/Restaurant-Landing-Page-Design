who we are

Starting with a speciality restaurant in India in 1997, the Group today has more than 250+ successfully operating restaurants across the globe.Considered as one of the fastest growing companies in India, the Group is spearheaded to becoming a major player in the economic scenario of India. Apart from India, it has a strong presence in countries like USA, Canada, UK, UAE and Australia.
